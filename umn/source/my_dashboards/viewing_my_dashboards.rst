Viewing My Dashboards
=====================

After creating dashboards, you can view all of them in your account on the **My Dashboards** page.

Procedure
---------

#. Log in to the management console.
#. In the upper left corner, select a region and project.
#. Choose **Service List** > **Cloud Eye**.

4. Click **My Dashboards**.

   -  Searching for dashboards: In the search box above the list, select a property and enter a keyword to search for the target dashboard.

      You can search for dashboards by **Name**, **Created by**, **Created**, or **Enterprise Project**.

   -  Adding dashboards to favorites: Locate the dashboard you want to add and click |image1| in the **Favorites** column.

      Then, you can select the **Display favorites only** checkbox in the upper right corner to display all favorite dashboards.

   -  Switching the dashboard view: Click |image2| in the upper right corner of the list to display dashboards in a list. You can also click |image3| to display dashboards in a view.
