Why Can't I Create Another Agency?
==================================

Your quota may be used up. In this case, you can delete unneeded agencies first or request a higher agency quota. Then you can use the agency to restore the Agent configuration. For details about how to adjust quotas, see :ref:`Quota Adjustment <en-us_topic_0154940152>`.
