Viewing Alarm Templates
=======================

#. Log in to the management console.

#. In the upper left corner, select a region and project.

#. Choose **Service List** > **Cloud Eye**.

#. Choose **Alarm Management** > **Alarm Templates**.

   On the **Alarm Templates** page, you can view the default or custom metric or event templates. You can also create, view, modify, delete, import, or export custom templates.
